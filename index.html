Summary:  Effects of Latitude on Weather

This project randomly selected over 500 cities around the globe and evaluated various weather metrics in relation to each cities latitude. It was posited that certain weather conditions like temperature may be directly correlated to distance from the equator.

The results were an interesting mix ranging from clear relationships to less obvious, possible connections. Surely, other factors are at play in some instances. Check out the plots to investigate further!
