Humidity vs. Latitude

Humidity may appear non-descript at first glance, but closer review shows bimodal clustering where humidity recordings reach their absolute lowest at a certain distance from the equator.

While interesting, there are clearly other influences at play given the presence of high humidity at all latitudes.
