Temperature vs. Latitude

Temperature reveals the clearest relationship to latitude with a visually parabolic curve. As cities near the equator (latitude 0), recorded temperatures increase.

The arrangement of temperature recordings is sensible given the earth's tilt and the area around the equator receiving more direct sunlight.
